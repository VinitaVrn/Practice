Chemistry Assignment

Chemistry is a branch of science concerned with the substances of which matter is composed, the investigation of their properties and reactions, and the use of such reactions to form new substances.

Chemistry Elements

The Periodic table is a tabular arrangemnet of chemical elements. Each element is represented by its chemical symbol such as Fe for iron, H for hydrogen, O for Oxygen. Elements are classified into groups or periods based on their properties.

Chemical Equations

Chemical reaction are represented using chemical equations. here's an example of chemical reaction:
2H2 + O2 -> 2H2O
This equation represent the reaction between hydrogen gas (H2) and Oxygen gas (O2) to form water (H2O).

Important Concepts

Stiochiometry is a collection of reactants and products in chemical reactions. It involves balabcing chemical reation equation to ensure mass and charge are conserved.
Subsripts indicates the number of atoms of each element in a molecule, while supercriptrepresent charges or no. of atoms in an ion.
